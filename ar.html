Immersive AR Experience

Explore the wonders of our solar system and beyond with our cutting-edge Augmented Reality (AR) app. Step into an immersive universe where you can discover over 20 planets, each with stunning visuals and fascinating facts. Dive deeper into the cosmos with interactive 3D models of select planets, allowing you to manipulate and examine every detail. Leveraging advanced ARKit/ARCore technology, our app seamlessly blends education and entertainment, utilizing machine learning algorithms to optimize performance and deliver a breathtaking experience. Powered by Unity3D and integrated with spatial audio, our app transports users to the farthest reaches of the galaxy, making learning about planetary science an unforgettable adventure.
